Clinical signs and symptoms of influenza information for health professionals.

Vaccine Product Information


Information for health care providers about vaccine supply and distribution.




Clinical Testing and Diagnosis

Antiviral medications are an important adjunct to influenza vaccine in the control of influenza.

Influenza (Flu) 






Influenza (flu) is a contagious respiratory illness caused by influenza viruses that infect the nose, throat, and lungs.
